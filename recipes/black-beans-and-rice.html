Black beans and rice



Description


Black beans and rice make a great 30-minute vegetarian meal that's filling, delicious, and cheap! Great as a side dish or in a burrito bowl, too.


Prep time: 5 min
Cook time: 25 min
Total time: 30 min
Servings: 6

Ingredients

  • 1 tespoon olive oil
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 3/4 cup uncooked white rice
  • 1 1/2 cups low sodium, low fat vegetable broth
  • 3 1/2 cups cannes black beans, drained
  • 1 teaspoon ground cumin
  • 1/4 teaspoon cayenne pepper

Instructions


  1. Step 1

    2. Heat oil in a saucepan over medium-high heat. Add onion and garlic; cook and stir until onion has softened, about 4 minutes. Stir in rice to coat; cook and stir for 2 minutes.

  2. Step 2

    3. Add vegetable broth and bring to a boil. Cover, reduce to a simmer, and cook until liquid is absorbed, about 20 minutes.

  3. Step 3

    4. Stir in beans, cumin, and cayenne; cook until beans are warmed through.
